Risotto

Description

Is roasted asparagus from the oven, yum.

Ingredients

  • Asparagus, trimmed
  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic, minced
  • Sea salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Lemon juice

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  3. Mix asparagus with ingredients
  4. Bake asparagus for 12 to 15 minutes
  5. Serve and enjoy
