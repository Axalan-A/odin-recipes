Risotto

Description

A creamy dish of rice flavored with mushrooms. A good compliment to mains such as roast beef, chicken, pork, or beef.

Ingredients

  • Broth
  • Olive Oil
  • Mustrooms
  • Shallots
  • Rice
  • Wine
  • Butter
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Chives

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Cook mushrooms until soft
  3. Add rice, cook and stir until coated with oil
  4. Add broth 1/2 cup at a time until rice is tender yet firm to the bite
  5. Remove from heat and stir in mushrooms, butter, chives, and cheese
  6. Season with salt and pepper and serve
