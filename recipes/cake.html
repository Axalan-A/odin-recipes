Texas Sheet Cake

Description

A perfect dessert for your next party, potluck, or holiday get together.

A dense chocolate cake topped with a fudgy pourable icing.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • White sugar
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Sour cream
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Water
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Preheat pan to 350 degrees F
  3. Make cake
  4. Make icing
  5. Add icing onto cake
  6. Serve and eat cake
